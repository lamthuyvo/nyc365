A world rocked

New beginnings are always harder than we think. From afar, they seem exciting and full of possibilities. But then we have to get used to a new environment, get to know new colleagues and make new friends. The process of unlearning old habits and learning new ones can be more painful than we initially anticipate.

Stephanie faced three major life changes when she moved to New York from San Francisco in 2014. A brutal winter made her miss the West Coast. She started a new exciting but also challenging job. And then, a few months into her first year in the city, her live-in boyfriend broke up with her.

An audio storyteller by trade, she found solace in music. This is the story of Stephanie’s first year in New York as told through her most played songs.
