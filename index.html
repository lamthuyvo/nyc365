On rocky beginnings

New beginnings are always harder than we think. At first, they seem exciting and full of possibilities but often we hit a few bumps

Stephanie faced a number of life changes when she moved to New York from San Francisco in 2014. An audio storyteller by trade, she found solace in music. This is the story of Stephanie’s first year in New York as told through her most played songs.

These are all the songs Stephanie listened to more than 10 times during her first year in New York.

Blue dots ● represents songs that she associated with sadness, ● are songs that she listened to with mixed feelings and pink dots ● are songs that made her happy.

In some ways, music was a way to deal with the inevitable loneliness that she sometimes felt in this new city. On the x-axis you can see days pass during her first year. On the y-axis you can see what time of the day she listened to them. See how she slowly but surely falls in love with the city?

What made this transition harder than anything was a breakup. Her boyfriend, who moved with her to New York from the West Coast, broke up with her halfway through her first year.

Heartbreak dance party!

What followed was a musical exercise in self-healing. These are all the songs she associated with her breakup. Let's see what music helped Stephanie through her breakup.
